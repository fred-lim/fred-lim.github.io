### Exercise 1.1a

<div class="solution">
    <div class="solution-header">Solution:</div>
    \begin{align*}
    \langle j \rangle =& \frac{14+15+3(16)+2(22)+2(24)+5(25)}{14} \\
    =& 21 \\
    (\langle j \rangle)^2 =& (21)^2 \\
    =& 441 \\
    \langle j^2 \rangle =& \frac{196+225+3(256)+2(484)+2(576)+5(625)}{14} \\
    =& \frac{3217}{7}
    \end{align*}
